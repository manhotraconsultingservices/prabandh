Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before making the switch.

How long does it take to get started on Prabandh? +
Most businesses are live within 1–2 business days. Our onboarding team helps you set up your company profile, import your party master and item catalogue, and configure user roles. The trial starts immediately — no installation, no server setup.
Can I migrate data from my existing software or Excel? +
Yes. Prabandh supports industry-standard XML export for ongoing reconciliation, and our team assists with importing party masters, item catalogues, and opening balances from Excel. You don't need to start from scratch — we meet you where your data is.
Is Prabandh compliant with Indian GST regulations? +
Absolutely. Prabandh was built ground-up for Indian GST compliance. HSN/SAC codes are locked in the item master. CGST/SGST vs IGST is auto-calculated based on party state and company GSTIN. GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B summaries are generated with one click, pre-reconciled against your books.
What happens if my business needs change and I need more users? +
You can upgrade your plan at any time from the Settings → Subscription screen. Your data is preserved, your users continue uninterrupted, and billing is prorated for the remaining period. Downgrading follows the same seamless process at next renewal.
How does role-based access control work? +
Prabandh offers 10 predefined role profiles: Admin, Enterprise Admin, Manager, Accountant, Sales Staff, Warehouse, HR, Executive, View Only, and Employee. Each role sees only the modules relevant to their function — Sales Staff cannot see HR or Accounting. App-level administrators can further customise module access per role for each company.
Is my business data secure on Prabandh? +
Yes. All data is transmitted over TLS 1.3 encryption. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text). Each company's data is strictly isolated — no company can access another's records. JWT authentication tokens expire after 7 days. A complete audit log tracks every user action in the system.
Can I manage multiple companies from one account? +
Yes. Prabandh is built for multi-company management from day one. Each company has its own isolated data, its own users and roles, and its own GST registration. An Application Administrator can oversee all companies, configure permissions, and manage subscriptions from a dedicated Admin Portal.
What support is available after I sign up? +
Trial and Standard plans include email support with a 24-hour response SLA. Professional plans get a dedicated account manager plus phone and WhatsApp support. Enterprise plans receive SLA-backed 24/7 support with a dedicated implementation team for onboarding, training, and custom requirements.
